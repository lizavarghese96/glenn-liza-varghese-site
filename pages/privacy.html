Privacy

This site includes an optional AI assistant. Your privacy matters. Here’s what to expect.

What the chat collects

  • Messages you type: When you use the chat, your prompt is sent to an AI service to generate a reply.
  • No account required: The chat works without login. Avoid sharing sensitive personal or confidential information.

AI service

The assistant can be powered by OpenAI. Prompts and generated responses may be processed by OpenAI to provide the service. See OpenAI’s privacy policy for details.

What we store

  • On this site: This static site does not store chat transcripts by default.
  • In your browser: We save UI preferences (theme mode, scroll mode) in local storage.

Your choices

  • Use the chat only if you’re comfortable. You can close it anytime.
  • Don’t include sensitive data (e.g., passwords, personal identifiers, proprietary secrets).
  • Switch themes and motion preferences to suit your needs.
